  • Space required is less as compared to other types of blender.
  • The machine basically consists of shell welded with conical octagonal shape ends with sturdy support on both sides.
  • Material handling in Octagonal blender is either through bin charging or vacuum charging system.
  • Bin charging ensures Dust free closed system for charging and discharging of product.
  • Rectangular opening lid with removable baffle mounted inside.

FLUID BED DRYER(FBD)

  • Capacity 300 gm –500 kg
  • Fluid bed processing involves drying, granulation and coating of particulate materials obtained from Rapid Mixer Granulator.
  • Uniform processing conditions are achieved by passing hot air through a product layer under controlled velocity conditions to create a fluidized state.
  • For many years, top spray and bottom spray was preferred, but now advantages of tangential spraying have become clearer.
  • Multiple bowl options are available for drying and processing.
  • Improved filter bag shaking assembly system with air purging operation.
  • Advanced WIP system with multiple dynamic wash points for thorough cleaning.
  • Hinged bowl system for easy manoeuvring.
  • Optional isolation valves provided for explosion suppression.
  • HEPA filtered & humidity-controlled air for drying operation.
